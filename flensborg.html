Flensborg Plantage

Wander through pine-scented trails, encounter peaceful wildlife and discover the hidden gem of Flensborg Plantage - a secluded lake, where you can enjoy a peaceful escape and find serenity in the water's edge.

From here on, there are several trails to take inside the forest, all varying in length, the red green and blue route. The routes are 1.5 km, 2.2 km and 4 km long respectively, and following all routes, you will be able to find the hidden lake inside the plantation, walking along the western routes.

Getting there

Park the car by Støvhøj, 3 km from Ulbjerg. On Tingvej, after entering the Plantation, there are several unofficial parking spots in the forest along the road. They are not marked, so be observant for openings in the forest to park the car.

Flora and fauna

In this beautiful, serene forest, which some call a hidden natural wonder, you will be met with the luscious smell of pine trees and moss alike, while hearing the songs of birds chirping.

If you're lucky, you might even catch a glimpse of a deer relaxing and drinking a sip of water by the lake.

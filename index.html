What is Fjordklyngen?

Fjordklyngen is a village association between Låstrup, Nr. Rind, Skals, Skringstrup, Sundstrup, and Ulbjerg – six small villages located in the northwestern corner of Viborg municipality.

The villages are geographically close to each other, situated in the middle of a fantastic natural area with two river valleys, heath, forest, and fjord.

Fjordklyngen works on the development of the area, focusing on culture, nature tourism, settlement, and business development.

What is the purpose of this site?

Fjordklyngen aims to spread awareness of its breathtaking natural areas, and through this site, it not only wishes to inform but also to enrich the experience of exploring the landscapes in and around Fjordklyngen.

This website serves as a digital gateway for nature enthusiasts, local residents, and tourists alike, offering a detailed glimpse into the serene trails, diverse ecosystems, and the hidden gems that await discovery.
